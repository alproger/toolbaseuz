Image Compression Optimization Tips

Learn how to compress images without losing quality for faster loading and smaller files.

2026-05-03 5 min

Why Compress Images

Smaller images load faster, reduce bandwidth and improve website performance.

Compression Strategy

Use moderate compression settings and preview the result before download.

Format Choices

WebP often gives the best size-to-quality ratio, while PNG is ideal for transparency.

Frequently asked questions

Should I always use WebP?

WebP is great for web use, but choose PNG for transparent images.

Will compression hurt quality?

Balanced settings preserve visual quality while reducing size.
