Resize photos to the right dimensions for social posts, documents and web pages.

Choosing Dimensions

Select dimensions based on the platform or document type you need.

Maintaining Quality

Keep aspect ratio locked when possible and avoid enlarging small images.

Practical Formats

Use JPG for photos, PNG for transparency and WebP for balanced web performance.

Frequently asked questions

Should I resize before uploading?

Yes, resizing before upload ensures faster loading and smaller file sizes.

Does aspect ratio matter?

Yes, preserving aspect ratio prevents distortion.
