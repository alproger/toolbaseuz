Image Crop Guide: Frame the Perfect Shot

Use cropping to remove distractions and improve composition for photos and graphics.

2026-05-03 5 min

Why Crop Images

Cropping focuses attention on the subject and removes unwanted background elements.

Best Composition

Use aspect ratios that match your destination, such as square for profiles or 16:9 for banners.

Tool Tips

Select the crop area, preview the result, and export the trimmed image for use.

Frequently asked questions

Should I crop before editing?

Yes, crop first to preserve the final composition.

Can I change the crop later?

You can re-crop the original image if needed.
