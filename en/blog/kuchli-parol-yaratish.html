Strong Password Creation Tips

Generate and manage strong passwords for secure accounts and digital privacy.

2026-05-03 5 min

What Makes a Password Strong

Strong passwords include length, mixed case, numbers, and special characters. Avoid common words or predictable patterns.

Using a Generator

Password generators create random strings that are hard to guess. Choose a length of at least 12 characters for most accounts.

Safe Password Practices

Use unique passwords for each account, store them in a password manager, and enable two-factor authentication where available.

Frequently asked questions

How long should passwords be?

Aim for 12 characters or more with varied character types.

Are generated passwords stored?

No, the generator produces passwords locally in your browser.
