PDF Editor Guide: Annotate and Update Documents

Edit PDF files with comments, text fields and annotations in the browser.

2026-05-03 5 min

Editing PDF Online

Use browser-based PDF editors to add notes, fill forms and mark up documents without desktop software.

Common Actions

Add text, highlight sections, draw shapes and sign documents directly in the PDF editor.

Best Practice

Keep a backup of the original PDF and export the updated version only after completing edits.

Frequently asked questions

Can I edit existing text?

Mostly you can add annotations and text overlays. Full OCR editing may not be supported.

Do I need an account?

No, many online PDF editors work without signing in.
