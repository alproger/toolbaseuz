Frequently asked questions

Is hashing done locally?

Yes, all hashing is performed in your browser without sending data anywhere.

Can I hash long text?

The tool supports normal-length text inputs for hashes.

Are hashes reversible?

No, MD5 and SHA-256 are one-way hashes and cannot be reversed.
