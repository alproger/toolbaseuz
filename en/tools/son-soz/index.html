Supported formats

Standard: integers and decimals — 1500 → One thousand five hundred. Currency mode: 1500.50 → One thousand five hundred dollars and fifty cents. Negative numbers ham qo‘llab-quvvatlanadi.

Maksimal qanday son kiritish mumkin?

999,999,999,999 (nine hundred ninety-nine billion). Covers all amounts found in financial documents.

Kasrli sonlar qanday yoziladi?

Use dot (.) or comma (,): 3.14 or 3,14. Max 2 decimal places.
