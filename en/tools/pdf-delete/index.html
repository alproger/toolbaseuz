Qanday foydalanish?

Visual selection usuli:

  1. Upload a PDF file — pages appear as thumbnails.
  2. Click the pages you want to delete (red = will be deleted).
  3. "Delete selected pages" click the button.
  4. Resultni yuklab oling.

Raqam bilan tanlash usuli:

  1. Upload a PDF file.
  2. "Raqam bilan tanlash" tabini bosing.
  3. Enter page numbers in the "From" and "To" fields.
  4. "Select" click the button — pages avtomatik tanlanadi.
  5. "Delete selected pages" click the button.
